Data Analysis · DOLE Region XI · 2025

Youth City Labor Market Analysis

Consolidated and analyzed employment data across Davao City for regional labor market planning — covering graduate distribution, employer profiling, and job vacancy matching. Produced data visualizations using Python to surface actionable insights for government decision-makers.

14,104 Graduates covered
13,138 Job vacancies analyzed
671 Companies profiled
  • Data consolidation from partner schools (CHED RO XI / PESO)
  • Graduate distribution by field of study (male / female / total)
  • Job vacancy analysis per industry sector
  • Employer profiling across 671 Davao City companies
  • ARIMA-based forecasting for employment trends
  • Final report and visualization deliverables
Web Development · Content Creation · DOLE XI

GIP & PHILJOB Quick-Guide Website

Designed and developed a multi-page guide website to improve public access to the Government Internship Program (GIP) and PHILJOB registration. The site reduced inquiry handling time significantly by providing clear, self-service navigation for applicants. Complemented by printed flyers for in-office distribution.


Outcome

Significantly reduced inquiry handling time. Multi-page navigation covering GIP overview, application steps, and post-registration instructions — complemented by printed flyers for in-office distribution.

  • GIP program overview & eligibility page
  • Step-by-step application guide
  • PHILJOB registration walkthrough
  • Post-registration instructions section
  • Flyer design for in-office distribution
  • Multi-page navigation structure
